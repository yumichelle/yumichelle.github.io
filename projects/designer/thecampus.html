The Campus Rate Card

challenge
Media kits done to catch people's attention and represent CUNYs' oldest running publication.

solution
This media kit is supposed to embody the feelings of the school. I chose the images of walls because it reminded me of the walls from the college campus. The college logo colors are purple and gray, so I used those colors for the text.
