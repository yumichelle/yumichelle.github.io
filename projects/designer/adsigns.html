Ads / Signs

challenge
To bring awareness to the advertiser's presence. To see the the top ad: click. ​2nd ad: click.

solution
I used Google Web Designer to design the ad to promote my portfolio as a graphic designer. This ad size is a good fit to put on top of websites. Since I am introducing myself as a graphic designer, I decided to use a slide effect for my name and logo. Because it would be harder to see the designs at such small size, I decided it would be wise to show a few examples and zoom out on the each project so the ad could get to the point and still retain audience retention.
I used Google Web Designer to create the book ad. It's short and simple enough to pull people in but adds a bit of a personal touch which reflects the book. I felt if added too much information, it would put people off.
New York City Restroom Initiative: The client wanted something that had a MTA feel and something familiar to people. The font used was Helvetica since it is currently used officially by the MTA. The logo was used as a banner to promote their initiative.

Tools used: Illustrator, Google Web Designer.
