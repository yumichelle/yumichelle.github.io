ICFJ Proposal

The International Center for Journalists (ICFJ) is a nonprofit shaping the future of quality journalism. This proposal was for a awards dinner.

challenge
Create pieces to represent ICFJ in a innovative way. No guidelines were given for the stationary.

solution
The globe is supposed to represent the travel of news across the world and the typography set-up was meant to be innovative much like ICFJ aims to possibly be. Typography is the main focus for this project because like journalism and writing, words are used. I wanted to put more emphasis on what you can do with words and letters.
