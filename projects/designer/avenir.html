Adrian Frutiger

challenge
Create an exhibition poster that embodies Avenir. The exhibition is for Avenir's creator, Adrian Frutiger.

solution
Tabloid sized posters for the type designer of Avenir. I only used Avenir as the type for these posters. Considering Avenir means "future" and that it was developed in 1988, I went for a future and/or contemporary look. He once said, "I always had human nature in mind."
What I have been noticing is the usage of 3D films and redefining stereotypes. For the first poster, my goal was to make the title pop, thus making a somewhat 3D effect. For the second poster, I wanted to create a new kind of look for posters. The color pink is usually associated with girls, but things are different now. Boys can like pink and princesses, girls can diversify a male-dominated career field, people of all genders can shave if they want to, and so on. This is the future I see. And the future is now.
