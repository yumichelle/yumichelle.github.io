The Amazing Book Is Not On Fire

challenge
The typography was too bright and exhausting to look at printed, and could have better representation for the authors since the book was about their lives. The good points of the book were the interaction between the typography and the people.

solution
This is a personal project. I took their book cover and edited it to show the image without the text. With references from cardboard stand pictures people have taken, I used it to fill in the gaps of places the book title has covered. I would like to thank Dan's black aesthetic. 10/10 from me.
This is my concept for their book cover. I wanted the title to look handwritten, interactive, and messy. While keeping that interaction from the original cover, I chose a type that looked handwritten to keep things personal. Dan and Phil make you feel like their friends, so I think having the title handwritten and interactive adds a personal touch and creates the feeling of closeness. If you have watched their YouTube videos, the rooms they film in seems orderly while it might not be (they have joked about it). Dan has recorded a video about it in 2013. There is also a joke about them being a mess (but also them causing others to be an emotional mess). The original cover title was bright and colorful. I decided to keep that colorfulness to represent Phil but tone it down a bit for Dan. The "The" part of the title forms the iconic cat whiskers. As a joke, I put "the world of Dan & Phil" in brackets to give a nod to Dan's first video. In a very abstract way, the title also forms a person. They do this "this year's insert-yourself-into-Dan-and-Phil-at-Vidcon photo." It doesn't work out too well because of Dan's hand, but in some ways it can.
