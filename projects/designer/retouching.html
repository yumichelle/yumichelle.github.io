Image Manipulation

challenge
Stock images gathered from DeviantArt and Google Images. The images below are Before and Afters respectively. The goal was to create a different feeling from the original image.

solution
I edited images around the themes of my interest (space, Game of Thrones, and Alice in Wonderland/ Kingdom Hearts). When I think of space, I think of wonder. But what if there were no stars or bright objects and the wonder was dimmed? In another curious note, what if Patrick won the “Game of Thrones”? And what if Alice, the curious girl that she is, wasn’t so curious? What kind of events would go on in your head if these images happened in their respective stories?
