kiba.

challenge
create a branding identity and packaging for kiba., a zine of dogs for a Japanese/English audience.

solution
I had to pick a font that was friendly to the Japanese writing system. The logo is to portray the zine as something pleasing to look at but also friendly by feeling personal and informal. Next is the interior signage. I envisioned kiba. being under the parent company, which is my company. Thus, I kept the typography the same. We have the big floor number on the left side of the circle, which is the directory of the rooms. The circle represents the period in kiba. and shows all of the rooms on the floor (the third floor in this case based on the graphics).
What is a company without a few company merchandise? I created postcards of my dog, Kiba, who kiba. is based on. For postcards, I hope to use consumers' own personal static images of their dogs to drive engagement and performance. I created a temporary landing page mock up on Sketch to get news on the zine and products.

Tools used: Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Sketch
